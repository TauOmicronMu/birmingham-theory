Events

The group organizes many regular events.

  • Our regular theoretical computer science seminar series takes place Fridays during term, hosting distinguished visiting researchers from around the world.
  • An informal weekly lab lunch gives a venue for both visitors and group members to present their recent work.
  • We are one of the main organizers of the annual Midland Graduate School in the Foundations of Computing Science.
  • We are one of the core members of the Yorkshire and Midlands Category Theory Seminar (YAMCATS).
  • The department seminar series often hosts talks on theoretical topics.
